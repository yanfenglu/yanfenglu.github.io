Summary

I have finished various works in the past. But since the pressure was not high, publication hasn't been my focus. Now that I have entered the realm of research I will have more works published and share them with similar mind. These include some works done before. But still there are many which are not suitable for publication, at least for the moment, for various reasons. Here is a selected list arranged by different areas.
